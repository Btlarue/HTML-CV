3D Printing

I began 3D Printing in December 2022. I am very much a beginner, with every failure and mistake I learn a little more! I have the base Ender 3, and so far I have over 200 print hours after upgrading several components:

  • Dual Z-axis rods
  • Ender Sprite extruder
  • CR-Touch auto bed level sensor
  • Filament runout sensor
  • Flexible bed sheet
I have plans to install a silent motherboard, and a Raspberry Pi with Octoprint OS to enable network control over my printer.
